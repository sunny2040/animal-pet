About Us

Who are we?

AnimaPet is an organization that provides guidance on pet adoption and care. AnimaPet organizes pet adoption events and donation drives to support animals in shelters or on the streets. Our mission is to: Clarify doubts about adoption and donations, Promote NGOs and websites that aim to help abandoned animals, Share news about pets, and Educate readers on how to care for their pets.

Adoption

Why adopt a pet?

If you want to adopt a pet and help us continue rescuing animals, please schedule a visit to our institution. Whether adopting or just spending time with the animals, you can contact us in whichever way you prefer. Most of the time, we rescue animals that have been abandoned or mistreated, so they tend to be more affectionate towards their owners, developing a great sense of loyalty and gratitude. We appreciate your support in advance.
